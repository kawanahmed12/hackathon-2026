# Centauri Digital Skills Pathway

Centauri is an accessible digital opportunity platform designed to help young people from underserved communities gain access to digital skills, mentorship, employability support, and technology resources.

Built during a university hackathon, the platform combines structured learning pathways, mentorship, accessibility tools, and AI-assisted guidance into one modern ecosystem designed to reduce barriers to entry into tech and digital careers.

---

## Problem

Many young people face barriers entering digital industries due to:

* lack of structured guidance
* limited access to mentorship
* low confidence
* lack of affordable learning pathways
* digital exclusion and accessibility challenges

Centauri was created to provide a free, beginner-friendly platform that helps young people start from zero and progress toward real opportunities.

---

## Features

### Skills Academy

* Structured beginner-to-advanced learning pathways
* Coding, AI, Marketing, Web Development, Design, and Video Editing tracks
* XP system, streaks, badges, and progress tracking
* Duolingo/Khan Academy-inspired learning experience

### AI Mentor Assistant

* AI-powered guidance prototype
* Course recommendations
* Career pathway suggestions
* Beginner-friendly support

### Mentorship Platform

* Mentor discovery and booking
* Career guidance
* Portfolio reviews
* CV support
* Mock interview sessions

### Accessibility Features

* Dyslexia-friendly mode
* Text resizing
* High contrast mode
* Text-to-speech support
* Keyboard accessibility improvements

### Device Access Support

* Device discovery and support pathways
* Community tech resources
* Digital inclusion focus
