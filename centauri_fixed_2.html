The honest problem

Talent is everywhere. Opportunity is rationed.

A teenager in a council estate has the same wiring as a teenager in a tech hub — but not the same laptop, the same Wi-Fi, the same uncle who works in product, or the same five hours a week of unstructured tutoring. Most "free" learning platforms assume you already own the runway. We don't. Centauri exists because skill alone never broke the ceiling — access did. Without devices, structured curricula, and warm introductions into industry, young people from underserved backgrounds stay locked out of the digital economy regardless of how hard they try.
